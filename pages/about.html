Shopmonkey exists to equip auto shops with software to run, understand and grow their business.

Our vision

We exist to help every shop thrive.

While we predominantly serve automotive repair, auto detail and heavy duty shops today, we also have bicycle , motorcycle, boat, marine shops and more who are changing their business with Shopmonkey. ‍ Our vision is to help every shop across a variety of related industries to thrive by helping them become more organized, reduce frustration and headache, and become more profitable while better serving their customers.

Our founder

Fueled by his passion for motorsports, cars, and technology, Shopmonkey was founded in 2016 by Ashot Iskandarian. Having worked at a number of software companies in Silicon Valley, he applied his product management expertise to begin building Shopmonkey. In 2017, Ashot and his wife took a leap of faith by quitting his job to focus on Shopmonkey to help empower and enable auto shops.

Our team

Headquartered in the Silicon Valley, we have a team of smart and humble people who have worked for some of the most recognized companies in the world. Our commitment is to serve our customers, which is why we pride ourselves in hiring the best talent regardless of their location to strategically grow a dynamic, globally distributed team.

maintenance for moms

Helping single moms pay for vehicle repairs since 2016

In 2016, Maintenance for Moms was birthed out of Shopmonkey Inc. The founder and his wife, Ashot and Annie Iskandarian, felt deeply compelled to integrate their faith in Jesus Christ and to express God’s love for single mothers and their children. Both Ashot and Annie have deeply personal connections to single mothers. The Iskandarians are committed to giving a portion of Shopmonkey profits and raising money to help single moms pay for their vehicle repairs. Shopmonkey and Maintenance for Moms partner together to love and serve this deserving group of women.
